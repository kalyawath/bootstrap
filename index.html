The Life of Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh (1853–1890)

was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter who became one of the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art.

In just over a decade, he created about 2,100 artworks, including around 860 oil paintings..

His Artistic Journey

Before becoming an artist, Vincent tried various careers as an art dealer and a preacher.

He began painting seriously at the age of 27.

He is best known for masterpieces like "The Starry Night" and "Sunflowers."

The Final Years:

Vincent struggled with severe mental illness throughout his life. In a moment of crisis, he famously cut off part of his own left ear.

In 1890, at the age of 37, he died from a self-inflicted gunshot wound.

he is now celebrated as a creative genius who paved the way for modern art.
